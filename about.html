My journey

I worked in government for almost 16 years before becoming a developer.

My background is mostly in an emergency services environment specialising in business process improvement, policy development and communications.

In early 2022 I decided it was time for a career change and hired a career consultant to help me on my journey.

After some research and self-analysis I applied for the She Codes Plus program, which I completed in May of 2023.

From there I began applying for jobs as a developer and started delving more into game development as a hobby.

This included attending workshops hosted by Level Her Up to learn more about the different aspects of game development.

I'm continuing online studies so you'll see projects added to my portfolio as I build my skills.
